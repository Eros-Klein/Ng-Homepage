Welcome to MyQuiz!

A webapp which lets you create and play 'MyQuizes' similar to kahoot!

Why use MyQuiz?

There are several of quiz-types to choose from (Single Answer, Multiple Answer, True or False, ...). You even have the possibility to create polls!

Limitations

Only selected individuals may create and start MyQuizes, however they do not have any limitations (infinite participants per quiz, ...)
